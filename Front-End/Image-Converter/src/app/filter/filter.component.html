With our current climate of easy access to information, we are faced with the problem of information overload. Therefore it is important that we are able to view data in a concise format. This will allow us to understand the data in a comprehensive manner without getting distracted by unnecessary information.

Graphing is a way to counter the problem effectively. However, mismatched or unclear graphs fail to help us understand the data adequately. This tool enables the standardization of graphs to solve this problem.

The Image converter hosts a variety of functions from analyzing graphs to outputting newly generated graph images.
