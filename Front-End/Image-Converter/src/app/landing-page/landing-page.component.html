What we offer

Content Management Made Easy

Core Idea

We offer content management services to MathU by shortening the content creation processes while making other processes multifunctional with the help of software. We also help maintain a high level of content quality.

Standardisation of Images

We help MathU content managers to maintain a set standard for their images, in terms of image quality, and size. The images used are solutions to mathematical graphing problems

Classification and Storage

Our system recognizes the types of graph images uploaded, aiding in the storage of these different images based on their classification
